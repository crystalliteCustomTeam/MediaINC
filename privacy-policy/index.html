2.2. Text / SMS Communications Terms & Conditions

Scope of SMS Communications

If you choose to opt-in to SMS communications from Infiniti Media Inc. you consent to receive text messages at the mobile number you provide. These messages may be automated and categorized as follows:

  • Transactional & Informational Messages – Includes order confirmations, delivery updates, account notifications, and customer support-related messages.
  • Marketing & Promotional Messages – Includes promotional offers, coupons, and marketing updates, but only if you have provided express written consent to receive such communications.

If you have opted in to receive only transactional and informational messages, you will not receive marketing messages. If you have expressly subscribed to promotional communications, you may receive both marketing and transactional messages.

2.3. Consent to Receive Automated Messages

You acknowledge that consent to receive automated text messages is not a condition of purchase or use of Infiniti Media Inc. services.

2.4. Message Frequency
  • Transactional Messages: Sent as needed, based on your order status, account activity, or customer service interactions.
  • Marketing Messages: Sent periodically based on promotions, special offers, or marketing campaigns (only if you have explicitly opted in).
  • Message frequency may vary.
2.5. Opt-Out and Support Options

You may opt out of receiving text messages at any time by texting "STOP". Upon receipt of your opt-out request, you will be unsubscribed and will no longer receive text messages from us.
